📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard | Power BI
📌 Project Overview

The Superstore Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping methods, and regional trends. The dashboard provides business stakeholders with actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

📊 Dashboard Components
1. KPI Cards

Provides a quick overview of business performance through key metrics.

2. Sales Trend Analysis

Compares monthly sales performance across different years to identify growth patterns and seasonality.

3. Profit Analysis

Tracks profit fluctuations and highlights profitable periods.

4. Category & Sub-Category Analysis

Identifies top-performing product categories and sub-categories.

5. Regional Performance

Visualizes sales distribution across regions and states.

6. Customer Segmentation

Analyzes revenue contribution from different customer segments.

7. Shipping Performance

Evaluates the effectiveness of shipping methods and delivery efficiency.

🎯 Business Insights
Consumer segment contributes the highest share of sales.
Office Supplies is the top-performing category.
Phones generate the highest sales among sub-categories.
The West region records the highest sales performance.
Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping method.
Sales and profit show noticeable growth toward the end of the year.
